Jiangtu Technology Launches FANUC Full-Series LCD Retrofit Solution

Shenzhen, China — May 1, 2026

Jiangtu Technology Co., Ltd., a leading provider of industrial video display integration solutions, today announced the launch of its comprehensive LCD retrofit solution covering the entire FANUC CNC display product line.

Comprehensive Compatibility

The new solution supports all major FANUC CRT display models, including:

  • A61L-0001-0074: 14-inch color CRT (FANUC 0i-M, 16i, 18i systems)
  • A61L-0001-0086: 14-inch color CRT (FANUC 0i-TC, 16i-B, 18i-B systems)
  • A61L-0001-0090: 9-inch monochrome CRT (FANUC 0-TC, 0-MC, 0i systems)
  • A61L-0001-0092: 9-inch monochrome CRT (FANUC 0i-M, 0i-TC systems)
  • A61L-0001-0093: 9-inch color CRT (FANUC 0i, 16i, 18i systems)
  • A61L-0001-0094: 14-inch color CRT (FANUC 0i-M, 16i-C, 18i-C systems)
  • A61L-0001-0095: 9-inch monochrome CRT (FANUC 0-TC, 0-MC systems)
  • D9MM-11A-0093: 9-inch monochrome CRT (FANUC 0-T, 0-M systems)

Key Features

  • Plug-and-Play Installation: All LCD modules retain the original CRT mounting hole pattern and HONDA 20-pin signal interface — zero programming, zero rewiring required
  • Industrial-Grade Reliability: Operating temperature -20°C to +60°C, IP65 front panel protection, MTBF ≥ 50,000 hours
  • Energy Efficiency: Up to 73% power reduction compared to original CRT displays
  • Enhanced Visibility: 350+ cd/m² brightness, 178° wide viewing angle, flicker-free LED backlight
  • 3-Year Warranty: Industry-leading warranty coverage with optional 5-year extension

Company Statement

"Our FANUC full-series LCD retrofit solution addresses the number one pain point for CNC machine shops — aging, unreliable CRT displays that cause unplanned downtime," said the Jiangtu Technology product director. "With our plug-and-play LCD modules, shops can eliminate display-related failures entirely while reducing energy costs and improving operator comfort."

Pricing and Availability

FANUC LCD retrofit modules are available immediately from Jiangtu Technology and authorized distributors. Volume pricing discounts are available for orders of 5 or more units. A trade-in program offers CNY 200-500 credit toward new LCD purchases when returning old CRT displays.

About Jiangtu Technology

Jiangtu Technology Co., Ltd. specializes in industrial video display integration solutions, including LCD display modules, video signal converters, touch screen solutions, and custom display integration services. With over 500 successful installations across China, the company serves the CNC machine tool, industrial automation, and manufacturing sectors. All products carry CE, UL, and ISO9001 certifications.
