About Kongto Technology

A technology-focused company specializing in industrial video display solutions provides display upgrade and retrofit solutions for CNC systems and industrial automation equipment. We specialize in upgrading and replacing old CRT screens with LCD displays for a full range of CNC machine tools from brands such as FANUC, Mitsubishi, TOTOKU, Siemens, HAAS, MAZAK, OKUMA, and MAKINO. Our products include industrial video signal conversion and industrial control dedicated displays.
