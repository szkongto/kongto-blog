About Kongto Technology

Kongto Technology Co., Ltd. is a technology-focused company specializing in industrial video display solutions. We provide display upgrade and retrofit services for CNC systems and industrial automation equipment.

We specialize in upgrading and replacing old CRT screens with modern LCD displays for a full range of CNC machine tools from brands such as FANUC, Mitsubishi, TOTOKU, Siemens, HAAS, MAZAK, OKUMA, and MAKINO. Our products include industrial video signal converters and industrial control displays.
