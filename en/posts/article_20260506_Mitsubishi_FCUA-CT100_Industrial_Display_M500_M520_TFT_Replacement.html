Mitsubishi FCUA-CT100 Industrial Display — The Display Core for M500/M520 CNC Systems

2026-05-06 Author: kongto01

The FCUA-CT100 is the core display interface for Mitsubishi M500/M520 CNC systems, responsible for signal transmission between the CNC controller and monitor. When the paired CRT monitor ages and fails, the FCUA-CT100 + MDT962B TFT combination provides a complete upgrade replacement solution — plug-and-play, thoroughly eliminating CRT aging and availability issues.

Why Choose FCUA-CT100 to Replace CRT?

After years of use, the original CRT monitors for M500/M520 systems commonly exhibit these issues:

IssueImpact
Electron gun agingScreen dims, contrast severely degraded
High-voltage pack failureUnable to power on or intermittent black screen
CRT tube burnoutComplete display failure, equipment downtime
Spare parts scarcityOriginal CRT discontinued for years, inflated prices

Installation Steps

Step 1: Remove Original CRT
Disconnect power, uninstall original CRT monitor. No need to touch system wiring.

Step 2: Install FCUA-CT100 + TFT Display
Use original mounting hole positions to secure new display, connect FCUA-CT100 signal cable.

Step 3: Power-on Test
After power-on, system auto-detects. Display quality surpasses original CRT.

Entire process requires no programming or rewiring — true "plug-and-play".
