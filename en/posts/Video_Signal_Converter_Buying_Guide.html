Selection Criteria by Application

For FANUC CNC Systems

Choose GBS-8219 for converting CGA/EGA signals from older FANUC systems to modern VGA displays. Supports 9" CRT replacement displays with native resolution.

For Siemens/Mitsubishi Systems

Choose KT-VGA-HDMI when legacy VGA output needs to connect to modern HDMI displays or monitors.

For HMI Integration

Choose KT-HDMI-VGA when modern HDMI-enabled HMI panels need to connect to VGA input industrial displays.
