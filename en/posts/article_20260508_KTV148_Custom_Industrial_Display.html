1. Product Overview

The KTV148 is a 12.1-inch custom industrial display from Shenzhen Jiangtu Technology Co., Ltd., featuring an industrial-grade TFT-LCD panel designed for CNC systems and industrial automation applications. This product breaks through standard size limitations, providing customized display solutions for legacy equipment upgrades and special workstation layouts.

As the standard-size model in the KTV series, KTV148 features a 12.1-inch display area, balancing display quality with installation space requirements—ideal for medium-sized CNC systems and industrial control panels.
