Product Overview

The GBS-8219 is an industrial-grade RGB to VGA video signal converter developed by Kongto Technology. Designed specifically to solve display interface incompatibility issues in legacy CNC control systems, it supports multiple legacy TTL signal inputs including CGA, EGA, MDA, and RGBS, outputting standard VGA (DE-15) signals at resolutions up to 1024x768@60Hz. Compatible with virtually all industrial VGA monitors on the market.

Installation Guide

Step 1: Identify Signal Type — Open the CNC system control cabinet, locate the monitor interface (typically DB15), and confirm the signal type (CGA/EGA/RGB) according to the pinout.

Step 2: Connect the Converter — Using a standard VGA cable, connect the converter output to the VGA monitor or Kongto Technology's compatible LCD display.

Step 3: Power On and Test — After powering on, the converter automatically identifies the input signal. The LED indicator lights up to indicate normal operation, and the monitor automatically displays the image.

Step 4: Secure the Device — Use the included mounting brackets or cable ties to fix the converter in an appropriate location inside the control cabinet, avoiding vibration and high-temperature areas.
