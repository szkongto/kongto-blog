FANUC A61L-0001-0093 LCD Display Professional Installation Guide

Published: 2026-05-03 | FANUC CNC Display Solutions

Product Overview

The FANUC A61L-0001-0093 is a professional-grade LCD display designed for FANUC CNC systems, particularly for Nanjing CNC equipment. This retrofit kit replaces aging CRT displays with modern LCD technology while maintaining 100% compatibility.

Key Specifications

  • Model: A61L-0001-0093
  • Type: Industrial LCD Display (replaces CRT)
  • Signal Interface: HONDA 20-pin
  • Power Input: DC 24V
  • Compatibility: FANUC 0i/16i/18i series CNC systems
  • Mounting: Identical to original CRT dimensions

Installation Precautions

Before starting the installation:

  • Power down the CNC system and disconnect main power
  • Wait 5 minutes for capacitors to discharge completely
  • Verify no voltage present before handling
  • Use anti-static wrist strap when handling electronic components
  • Follow all local electrical safety regulations

Step-by-Step Installation

Step 1: Remove the Existing CRT

Carefully disconnect the HONDA 20-pin signal cable and power connector from the back of the CRT. Remove the mounting screws (typically 4-6 screws) and gently slide the CRT unit out of the control cabinet.

Step 2: Prepare the LCD Kit

Unpack the A61L-0001-0093 LCD kit and verify all components are present. Check the LCD panel for any shipping damage. Confirm the HONDA 20-pin connector is intact.

Step 3: Install the LCD Panel

Position the LCD panel using the same mounting points as the original CRT. The A61L-0001-0093 is designed with identical dimensions - it's a direct drop-in replacement requiring no modifications to the cabinet.

Step 4: Connect Cables

Connect the HONDA 20-pin signal cable to the LCD interface. Connect the DC 24V power cable to the original power supply connector. Ensure all connections are secure and properly seated.

Step 5: Test and Verify

Restore power and boot the CNC system. Verify the display shows clear, stable images. Check all display functions including:

  • Text display clarity
  • Graphics and tool path display
  • Alarm messages and diagnostics
  • Touch screen functionality (if applicable)

Professional Tips

  • Take photos of the original wiring before disconnecting for reference
  • Label all cables during removal to ensure correct reconnection
  • Test the display for at least 30 minutes before closing the cabinet
  • Keep the original CRT as a spare or for future reference
