FANUC A61L-0001-0093 LCD Retrofit Solution

Published: 2026-05-03 Updated: 2026-05-15 Author: Kongto Technology Technical Department

I. Equipment Status and Original Specifications

The FANUC A61L-0001-0093 is one of the most widely used 9-inch monochrome CRT displays in FANUC CNC systems, commonly found in FANUC 0 series, 0i series, and OM-D CNC systems. This display uses cathode ray tube technology with a designed lifespan of approximately 15,000 hours (about 3-5 years under 3-shift operation).

Common Aging Faults

Units in service for over 5 years commonly exhibit the following issues:

  • Brightness Attenuation: CRT cathode ray tube aging causes brightness to drop by over 50%, making the screen difficult to view during daylight
  • Poor Focusing: Blurred character edges and distortion, leading to parameter reading errors during programming
  • Flickering and Jitter: Aging high-voltage circuits cause image instability, leading to operator fatigue during prolonged use
  • High-Voltage Safety Hazard: High-voltage package inside CRT (anode voltage approx. 10-15kV) poses electric leakage risk
  • Complete Blackout: Filament burnout or high-voltage package failure, rendering the equipment inoperable

IV. Installation Procedure (Approx. 30 Minutes)

  1. Power Off and Remove

    Cut off CNC system main power, remove 4 fixing screws from CRT display bezel, disconnect HONDA 20-pin signal cable and DC 24V power cable from the back.

  2. Install LCD Module

    Place the LCD retrofit module into the original CRT mounting position, align mounting holes, and secure with original screws (keyed design, cannot be installed incorrectly).

  3. Connect Cables

    Insert HONDA 20-pin signal cable into LCD module signal port (with keyed connector, cannot be inserted incorrectly), connect DC 24V power cable.

  4. Power On and Adjust

    Restore power supply, LCD displays automatically after system boot. Use OSD buttons on the front panel to fine-tune brightness, contrast, screen position and other parameters.

⚠️ Note: The entire installation process does not require any CNC system parameter modifications, no recalibration needed, and does not affect machine tool accuracy.

V. Usage Advantages and Maintenance Recommendations

Core Advantages

  • Plug-and-Play: No equipment structure modification needed, upgrade completed in 30 minutes
  • Improved Display Quality: High brightness, no flicker, wide viewing angle, adaptable to various lighting environments
  • Reduced Power Consumption: Power consumption is only 1/3 of original CRT, reducing CNC cabinet heat dissipation burden
  • Extended Lifespan: Industrial-grade LCD lifespan is 3x+ that of original CRT
  • No High-Voltage Hazard: Entire unit low-voltage powered, eliminating CRT high-voltage leakage safety risk

Long-Term Maintenance Recommendations

  • Periodically (every 6 months) check if signal cable connectors are loose
  • Maintain good ventilation in electrical cabinet, avoid long-term high-temperature operation
  • Periodically calibrate brightness and contrast through OSD menu
  • If display abnormality is found, prioritize checking if HONDA 20-pin connector is oxidized
