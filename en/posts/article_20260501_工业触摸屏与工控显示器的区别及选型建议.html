Industrial Display Procurement & Selection Guide

2026-05-01 作者: 江图科技

Keywords: industrial display selection, LCD procurement, CNC display specification, industrial monitor

1. Key Selection Criteria

CriterionDescriptionTypical Requirement for CNC
Display SizeScreen diagonal measurement8.4" - 15" (match original CRT)
ResolutionNative pixel count800x600 (SVGA) or 1024x768 (XGA)
BrightnessMaximum luminous output≥ 350 cd/m² for workshop environments
Contrast RatioDarkest vs brightest luminance≥ 800:1 for clear text readability
Operating TemperatureEnvironmental range-20°C to +60°C (wider preferred)
Protection RatingIngress protection (IP)IP65 front panel minimum
Signal InterfaceInput connector typeHONDA 20-pin (FANUC) / VGA / DVI
Power SupplyInput voltage and consumption24V DC, ≤ 15W
MTBFMean Time Between Failures≥ 50,000 hours
Viewing AngleMaximum angle for acceptable contrast≥ 170° horizontal/vertical
Response TimePixel transition speed≤ 8ms for dynamic CNC displays

4. Advanced Configuration Options

  • Touch screen integration: Resistive or capacitive touch overlay for HMI applications
  • Anti-glare treatment: Optical bonding with anti-reflective coating for high-ambient-light environments
  • Custom mounting: VESA or OEM-specific brackets for non-standard enclosures
  • Extended temperature range: -40°C to +85°C for extreme environments
  • Dual-display configuration: Mirror or extended desktop for multi-monitor CNC setups
