1. Product Overview

The FANUC A61L-0001-0093 LCD Display is a direct replacement for the original 9-inch CRT display used in FANUC CNC systems. This plug-and-play upgrade solution maintains full compatibility with existing machine installations while providing modern LCD technology benefits.

5. Installation Guide

  1. Power off the CNC machine and disconnect the display power cable
  2. Remove the original CRT: Unscrew 4-6 mounting screws and disconnect the HONDA 20-pin signal cable
  3. Clean the mounting surface with alcohol to remove oil and dust
  4. Install the LCD: Align with original mounting holes, the LCD snaps into position automatically
  5. Connect cables: Insert the HONDA 20-pin connector (align direction marker "1") and connect 24V DC power
  6. Secure with screws: Tighten to 4-5 N·m using a torque wrench
  7. Power on and verify: Check display resolution is 800x600 in FANUC system parameters
