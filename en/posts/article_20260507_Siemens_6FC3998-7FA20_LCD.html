Self-Diagnosis: Is Your CRT Failing?

If your Siemens CRT monitor shows any of the symptoms below, the CRT has reached end-of-life. Repairing aging CRTs is costly and yields only short-term results — replacement is the only reliable long-term solution. We recommend proactive replacement before the CRT completely fails to avoid production interruptions caused by sudden failures.

⏱️

Slow warm-up
Takes 5–10 minutes after power-on before the screen becomes readable; image remains unstable, requiring repeated adjustments of brightness and contrast to reach a barely usable state. This indicates severe degradation of the electron gun's cathode emission capability.

🔅

Excessive dimming
Overall image is dim; maxing out brightness and contrast does not help; screen appears yellowish or reddish — this is a typical sign of phosphor aging. Even with increased brightness, the image remains blurry.

↔️

Geometry distortion
Content scrolls vertically or distorts horizontally; image shifts with temperature changes; picture shows pincushion or barrel distortion. These phenomena indicate that the deflection coil and high-voltage circuit are unstable and may fail at any time.

🔊

Abnormal noise
High-pitched whining from the high-voltage transformer; sometimes a burning smell can be detected — this is a dangerous sign of insulation aging in high-voltage components, which may lead to breakdown. Continued use may cause safety accidents.

⬜

Partial/complete blackout
Only a single horizontal or vertical line visible, or no image at all; sometimes tapping the cabinet temporarily restores the image, but this indicates poor contact and means the monitor is on the verge of failure.

⚡

Flickering or jitter
Unstable image with visible flicker; character readability severely compromised; prolonged viewing causes eye fatigue and headaches. This not only affects work efficiency but may also cause permanent damage to the operator's vision.

The root cause of all these symptoms is cathode emitter depletion in the CRT electron gun — a natural aging process that cannot be reversed. The only durable fix is LCD replacement. Our LCD replacement kit uses brand-new industrial-grade LCD panels with uniform brightness, vivid colors, and long service life, perfectly replacing aging CRT monitors.

Professional Installation Guide

Proper installation is the key to ensuring long-term stable operation of the replacement kit. Please read the following installation steps carefully and strictly follow the procedures. If you have any questions about any step, please contact our technical support team at any time.

1

Safety Preparation

Turn off the main power switch of the CNC machine tool and wait at least 5 minutes to allow the high-voltage capacitor inside the CRT monitor to fully discharge. This is to ensure your safety — CRT has dangerous high voltage of 15-25kV inside, which may still retain dangerous voltage even after power off. Also prepare the following tools: Phillips screwdriver (#2), flathead screwdriver, anti-static wrist strap.

⚠️ Important: Please wear an anti-static wrist strap before operation to avoid electrostatic damage to electronic components.
2

Remove the CRT Monitor

Open the machine tool electrical cabinet door and locate the CRT monitor. Usually, the CRT monitor is fixed to the electrical cabinet door or internal mounting frame with 4 screws. First, take photos to record the position of all connected wires (especially power and signal wires), then carefully unplug all connected wires. Note: The high-voltage cap behind the CRT carries dangerous voltage, do not touch it directly with your hands.

💡 Suggestion: Use your phone to take photos to record the connection position of each wire, so you won't make mistakes when installing the new monitor.
3

Remove CRT Fixing Screws

Use a Phillips screwdriver to remove the 4 screws fixing the CRT monitor (usually located at the four corners). Since the CRT is heavy (about 8-10kg), it is recommended to have another person assist in supporting it to avoid personal injury or equipment damage caused by falling during the removal process. Carefully take out the CRT monitor from the installation position.

⚠️ Safety Warning: CRT contains lead and other harmful substances, please dispose of it according to local environmental protection regulations, do not discard it随意.
4

Install the LCD Replacement Kit

Take out our LCD replacement kit and check if the appearance is intact. Align the LCD module with the original CRT mounting holes and fix it with the included screws. Since our LCD module uses exactly the same mounting dimensions as the original CRT, you will find that no modifications are needed during installation, and the screw holes are perfectly aligned.

💡 Tip: Do not tighten the screws all at once. First install all screws, then tighten them evenly to ensure uniform panel stress.
5

Connect Power and Signal Wires

Connect the original CRT power wire to the power input terminal of the LCD replacement kit (supports DC 10-30V wide voltage input). Then connect the signal wire to the signal input terminal of the LCD module. Our kit is designed with an interface that is 100% compatible with the original CRT, and you will find that no wiring adapters or converters are needed when connecting.

✅ Check Point: Before connecting, please confirm again that the power polarity is correct (do not reverse positive and negative), and the signal wire is connected firmly.
6

Power-on Test

Reconnect the main power supply of the CNC machine tool and turn it on for testing. You will immediately see the LCD screen light up, displaying a clear and stable image. Check the following: Is the image clear? Is the brightness uniform? Is there any flicker? Are the touch buttons (if any) responsive? Usually, our LCD replacement kit works perfectly without any adjustment.

🎉 Congratulations: If everything is normal, your CRT upgrade replacement is successfully completed! The entire process usually takes only 15-30 minutes.
7

Finishing Work

After confirming that everything is normal through testing, organize the connected wires to ensure the wire harness is arranged neatly and does not hinder the normal closing of the machine electrical cabinet door. Properly package the removed old CRT monitor and dispose of it according to local environmental protection regulations. It is recommended to record the date and content of this modification in the machine tool maintenance log for future reference.

📝 Suggestion: Keep the product packaging and instruction manual so that you can process quickly when warranty service is needed.
