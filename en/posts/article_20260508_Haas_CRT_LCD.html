📅 2026-05-08 👤 Kongto Technology 🏷️ Haas | CRT Retrofit | LCD Replacement

1. Industry Pain Points of Aged CRT Monitors

Haas CNC machines are widely used in precision manufacturing workshops for reliable performance. However, most legacy units are equipped with outdated CRT monitors, which suffer from flickering screens, color distortion, brightness degradation, high-voltage failures and discontinued spare parts. Frequent blackouts and screen damage lead to unexpected machine downtime and production delays.

Traditional CRT monitors feature bulky size, high power consumption and poor heat dissipation. Original spare parts are no longer available, resulting in long maintenance cycles and high costs for manufacturing enterprises.
