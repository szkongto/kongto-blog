How Dual-Input Auto-Detection Works

The core advantage of KT819 is its intelligent dual-input interface design. It can simultaneously connect two different signal sources, and the device automatically determines which interface has an active signal input and switches to the corresponding mode:

  • VGA Pass-through Mode: When the VGA input detects a valid VGA signal (typically from modern devices), the converter directly passes the signal through to the VGA output with zero latency.
  • RGBS Conversion Mode: When the RGBS input detects a separate sync RGB signal (from legacy CNC equipment), the converter converts the RGBS signal to standard VGA timing and outputs it.
  • CVBS Mode: When composite video input detects a PAL/NTSC signal, it automatically decodes and up-converts to VGA output (this mode is optional).
