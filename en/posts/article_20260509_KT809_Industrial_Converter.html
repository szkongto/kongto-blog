Advantages of the Isolated Version (KT809-I)

KT809 is available in two versions: Standard and Isolated (KT809-I). The isolated version features a built-in high-speed optocoupler isolator, which is particularly important in the following scenarios:

  • Shared Ground Issues: When the converter connects two devices (signal source and monitor) with significant ground potential difference, isolation prevents ground loop current from damaging device interfaces.
  • Factory Power Grid Fluctuation Environments: Voltage fluctuations caused by the start/stop of large motors, welding machines, etc. in industrial power grids — the isolation module absorbs transient surges effectively.
  • Long-Distance Signal Transmission: When the monitor is far from the signal source (more than 5 meters), isolation design reduces signal attenuation and noise coupling.
  • Near Sensitive Equipment: Isolation design reduces the impact of electromagnetic radiation on converted signals.

Typical Applications

  • Legacy CNC Machine Display Upgrade: Compatible with CGA/EGA interfaces from various brands of legacy CNC systems, converting analog signals to VGA and connecting to modern industrial LCD displays.
  • Industrial Vision Inspection Systems: Convert non-standard industrial camera output to VGA, integrating into a unified display platform and reducing system integration costs.
  • PLC HMI Interface Expansion: Extend VGA interfaces for PLCs that only support legacy display output, compatible with modern HMI touchscreens or industrial monitors.
  • Equipment Retrofitting and Second-Hand Market: In second-hand equipment transactions, the buyer's monitor interface may not match the equipment. KT809 provides a plug-and-play compatibility solution.
