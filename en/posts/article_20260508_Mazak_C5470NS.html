Mazak C-5470NS CNC Lathe: CRT to LCD Display Upgrade Case Study

Published: 2026-05-08 | Source: Kongto Technology

Product Background

Mazak is a world-renowned Japanese CNC machine tool brand, and the C-5470NS CNC lathe is widely used in automotive parts manufacturing, precision machinery, and aerospace industries. The machine is equipped with a 9-inch color CRT display that serves as the primary interface for operators to monitor machining parameters and program status.

However, CRT technology has inherent aging problems. After prolonged use, the cathode ray tube easily deteriorates, leading to common failures such as: dim or dark screen, flickering, color distortion, blurry characters, and complete black screen. These issues not only disrupt production schedules but also pose safety risks from misreading parameter data.

Mazak C-5470NS CNC Lathe | Source: Kongto Technology

Our Solution

Kongto Technology provides professional CRT to LCD display upgrade services for the Mazak C-5470NS. We replace the aging CRT monitor with a high-brightness, high-reliability industrial-grade LCD screen. Key advantages include:

  • Plug and Play: Fully compatible with the original interface. No wiring modifications required. All original machine parameters are preserved.
  • Factory-Grade Quality: Strict adherence to original manufacturer standards. Every upgraded unit undergoes 72-hour burn-in testing.
  • Crystal Clear Display: Significantly improved resolution, 178-degree ultra-wide viewing angle, accurate color reproduction.
  • Rock-Solid Reliability: Strong anti-interference performance, suitable for complex industrial electromagnetic environments.

After upgrade, no parameter recalibration is needed. The machine is ready to use immediately, minimizing downtime and reducing maintenance costs significantly.
