Self-Diagnosis: Is Your SM0901-579417-TA Failing?

If your SM0901-579417-TA shows any of the symptoms below, the CRT has reached end-of-life. Repairing aging CRTs is costly and yields only short-term results — replacement is the only reliable long-term solution.

⏱️

Slow warm-up
Takes 5–10 minutes after power-on before the screen becomes readable; image remains unstable

🔅

Excessive dimming
Overall image is dim; maxing out brightness and contrast does not help; screen appears yellowish

↔️

Geometry distortion
Content scrolls vertically or distorts horizontally; image shifts with temperature changes

🔊

Abnormal noise
High-pitched whining from the high-voltage transformer or burning smell from aging components

⬜

Partial/complete blackout
Only a single horizontal or vertical line visible, or no image at all — complete display failure

⚡

Flickering or jitter
Unstable image with visible flicker; character readability severely compromised, causing eye strain

The root cause of all these symptoms is cathode emitter depletion in the CRT electron gun — a natural aging process that cannot be reversed. The only durable fix is LCD replacement. Our TFT LCD solution completely eliminates these issues with modern display technology.
