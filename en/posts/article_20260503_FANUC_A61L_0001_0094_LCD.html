FANUC A61L-0001-0094 14-inch Color LCD Retrofit Guide

Published: 2026-05-03 | FANUC CNC Display Solutions

Product Overview

The FANUC A61L-0001-0094 is a 14-inch color CRT display originally used in FANUC CNC systems, particularly for Nanjing CNC equipment. As these color CRT displays age, they suffer from color drift, brightness loss, and eventual failure. Our LCD retrofit kit provides a cost-effective, reliable solution with vibrant color reproduction and modern LCD technology.

Key Specifications

  • Model: A61L-0001-0094
  • Type: 14-inch color LCD (replaces CRT)
  • Resolution: 640×480 (VGA-compatible)
  • Interface: HONDA 20-pin signal connector
  • Power: DC 24V (compatible with original CRT power supply)
  • Compatibility: FANUC 0i/16i/18i series CNC systems
  • Brightness: 400 cd/m² (higher than original CRT)
  • Lifespan: 50,000+ hours (vs. 15-20 years for CRT)

Why Upgrade to Color LCD?

Color CRT displays have a typical lifespan of 15-20 years. As they age, you may experience:

  • Color accuracy degradation (reds appear orange, blues appear purple)
  • Reduced brightness and contrast (harder to read in bright workshops)
  • Flickering or image distortion
  • Complete failure due to cathode ray tube wear
  • Difficulty obtaining replacement parts (discontinued by manufacturer)

Color LCD technology solves these problems by providing stable, vibrant colors with a lifespan of 50,000+ hours. The A61L-0001-0094 retrofit kit maintains 100% compatibility with your existing CNC system while delivering superior image quality.

Installation Guide

Step 1: Safety Preparation

Power down the CNC system and disconnect the main power supply. Wait 5 minutes for capacitors to discharge completely. Verify no voltage present before proceeding. Always wear an anti-static wrist strap when handling electronic components.

Step 2: Remove Existing CRT

Carefully disconnect the HONDA 20-pin signal cable and power connector from the back of the CRT. Remove the mounting screws (typically 4-6 screws) and gently slide the CRT unit out of the control cabinet. Be careful not to damage the CRT glass.

Step 3: Prepare LCD Kit

Unpack the A61L-0001-0094 LCD kit and verify all components are present: LCD panel, mounting brackets, cables, and installation manual. Check the LCD panel for any shipping damage. Confirm the HONDA 20-pin connector is intact.

Step 4: Install LCD Panel

Position the 14-inch color LCD panel using the same mounting points as the original CRT. The A61L-0001-0094 is designed with identical dimensions - it's a direct drop-in replacement requiring no modifications to the cabinet or mounting holes.

Step 5: Connect Cables

Connect the HONDA 20-pin signal cable to the LCD interface. Connect the DC 24V power cable to the original power supply connector. Ensure all connections are secure and properly seated. The LCD kit is designed to work with the original power supply - no additional power adapters needed.

Step 6: Test and Verify

Restore power and boot the CNC system. Verify the display shows clear, stable, vibrant colors. Check all display functions including:

  • Text display clarity and color accuracy
  • Graphics and tool path display
  • Alarm messages and diagnostics (should display in correct colors)
  • Menu navigation and parameter settings
  • Backlight uniformity across the entire screen

Key Advantages

  • Direct Replacement: Same mounting dimensions as original 14-inch CRT - no drilling or modifications required
  • 100% Compatible: HONDA 20-pin interface matches original CRT specification
  • Superior Color: 256-color TFT LCD with LED backlight provides vibrant, accurate colors
  • Industrial Grade: Designed for 24/7 operation in harsh industrial environments (dust, vibration, temperature fluctuations)
  • Long Lifespan: LCD technology rated for 50,000+ hours (equivalent to 15+ years of normal use)
  • Energy Efficient: Consumes less power than CRT (reduced heat generation)
  • Warranty: 2-year warranty included

Professional Tips

  • Take photos of the original wiring before disconnecting for reference
  • Label all cables during removal to ensure correct reconnection
  • Test the display for at least 30 minutes before closing the cabinet
  • Keep the original CRT as a spare or for future reference
  • Clean the LCD screen with isopropyl alcohol and lint-free cloth only
