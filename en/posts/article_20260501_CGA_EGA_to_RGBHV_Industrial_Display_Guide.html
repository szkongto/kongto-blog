3. Retrofit Implementation Steps

3.1 Preparations

  • Site survey: Record display model, size, housing structure, power supply method
  • Equipment list: Converter, BNC-to-RCA cable, VGA-to-D-Sub cable, screwdriver, insulating tape
  • Power check: Confirm power voltage range (12-24 V)
  • Environmental assessment: Temperature, humidity, vibration

3.2 Hardware Wiring

  1. Disconnect the signal cable of the old display
  2. Connect the signal input to the converter
  3. Connect the converter output to the new RGBHV display
  4. Power on and test

4. Conclusion

Retrofitting CGA/EGA displays to RGBHV is a cost-effective solution for extending the life of industrial equipment. With the right converter and proper installation, you can achieve modern display compatibility at a fraction of the cost of replacing the entire display system.
