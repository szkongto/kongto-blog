3. Hidden Costs of CRT Repair

  • Production loss: Each unplanned CRT failure causes 2-8 hours of production downtime
  • Inventory risk: CRT tubes are increasingly scarce; lead times of 2-4 weeks for replacement tubes
  • Repeat failures: Repaired CRTs have a 40-60% failure recurrence rate within 12 months
  • Safety risk: CRT high-voltage circuits (25kV+) pose electrical hazard during repair
  • Environmental compliance: CRT disposal requires special handling for lead and phosphor waste

5. Recommendation

For FANUC CNC systems with CRT displays older than 8 years, LCD retrofit is the clear economic choice. The investment pays for itself within 12-18 months, and the long-term savings are substantial.
