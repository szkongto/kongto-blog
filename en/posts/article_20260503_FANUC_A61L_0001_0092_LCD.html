FANUC A61L-0001-0092 MDT947B 9-inch Monochrome LCD Retrofit Guide

Published: 2026-05-03 | FANUC CNC Display Solutions

Product Overview

The FANUC A61L-0001-0092 (MDT947B) is a 9-inch monochrome CRT display originally used in FANUC CNC systems. As these CRT displays age, they suffer from brightness loss, flickering, and eventual failure. Our LCD retrofit kit provides a cost-effective, reliable solution with modern LCD technology while maintaining 100% compatibility.

Key Specifications

  • Model: A61L-0001-0092 (MDT947B)
  • Type: 9-inch monochrome LCD (replaces CRT)
  • Interface: HONDA 20-pin signal connector
  • Power: DC 24V (compatible with original CRT power supply)
  • Compatibility: FANUC 0i/16i/18i series CNC systems

Why Retrofit?

Monochrome CRT displays have a typical lifespan of 15-20 years. Common issues include reduced brightness, flickering, and difficulty obtaining replacement parts. LCD technology solves these problems with a lifespan of 50,000+ hours.

Installation Guide

1. Power down CNC system. 2. Disconnect CRT cables. 3. Install LCD panel. 4. Connect cables. 5. Test display.
