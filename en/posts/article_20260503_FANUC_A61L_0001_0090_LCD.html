FANUC A61L-0001-0090 9-inch Monochrome CRT to LCD Retrofit Guide

Published: 2026-05-03 | FANUC CNC Display Solutions

Overview

The FANUC A61L-0001-0090 is a 9-inch monochrome CRT display originally designed for FANUC 0i/16i/18i series CNC systems. As these CRT displays age, they suffer from reduced brightness, flickering, and eventual failure. With original parts discontinued, LCD retrofit kits provide a cost-effective and reliable upgrade solution.

Product Specifications

  • Model: A61L-0001-0090
  • Type: 9-inch monochrome LCD (replaces CRT)
  • Interface: HONDA 20-pin signal connector
  • Power: DC 24V (compatible with original CRT power supply)
  • Compatibility: FANUC 0i/16i/18i series CNC systems
  • Mounting: Identical dimensions to original CRT for direct replacement

Why Retrofit?

CRT displays have a typical lifespan of 15-20 years. As they age, you may experience:

  • Reduced brightness and contrast
  • Flickering or image distortion
  • Complete failure due to cathode ray tube wear
  • Difficulty obtaining replacement parts (discontinued by manufacturer)

LCD retrofit kits solve these problems by replacing the aging CRT with modern LCD technology while maintaining 100% compatibility with your existing CNC system.

Installation Guide

Step 1: Safety Preparation

Power down the CNC system and disconnect the main power supply. Wait 5 minutes for capacitors to discharge. Verify no voltage present before proceeding.

Step 2: Remove Existing CRT

Carefully disconnect the HONDA 20-pin signal cable and power connector from the back of the CRT. Remove mounting screws and slide out the CRT unit from the control cabinet.

Step 3: Install LCD Kit

Position the LCD panel using the same mounting points as the original CRT. The A61L-0001-0090 LCD kit is designed with identical dimensions - it's a direct drop-in replacement requiring no modifications to the cabinet.

Step 4: Connect Cables

Connect the HONDA 20-pin signal cable to the LCD interface. Connect the DC 24V power cable. Verify all connections are secure before powering on.

Step 5: Test and Verify

Restore power and boot the CNC system. Verify the display shows clear, stable images. Check all display functions including text, graphics, and alarm messages.

Key Advantages

  • Direct Replacement: Same mounting dimensions as original CRT - no drilling or modifications required
  • 100% Compatible: HONDA 20-pin interface matches original CRT specification
  • Industrial Grade: Designed for 24/7 operation in harsh industrial environments
  • Long Lifespan: LCD technology rated for 50,000+ hours (vs. 15-20 years for CRT)
  • Warranty: 2-year warranty included
