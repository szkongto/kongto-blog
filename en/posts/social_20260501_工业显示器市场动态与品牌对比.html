Industrial Display Market Trends & Brand Comparison

1. Market Overview

The global industrial display market was valued at approximately USD 6.2 billion in 2025 and is projected to reach USD 9.1 billion by 2030, growing at a CAGR of 8.1%. Key growth drivers include:

  • Increasing adoption of Industry 4.0 and smart factory initiatives
  • Rising demand for retrofit solutions as legacy CRT displays reach end-of-life
  • Growing need for high-brightness, wide-temperature displays in harsh industrial environments
  • Expansion of touch screen HMI in manufacturing automation

2. Key Market Trends

2.1 CRT-to-LCD Retrofit Acceleration

An estimated 2.5 million CNC machine tools worldwide still use CRT displays. As CRT tubes and components become increasingly scarce, the CRT-to-LCD retrofit market is experiencing rapid growth. Key statistics:

  • CRT tube production has declined by 95% since 2010
  • Average CRT repair cost has increased 40% in the last 5 years due to parts scarcity
  • LCD retrofit adoption rate has grown from 8% in 2020 to 28% in 2025

2.2 Touch Screen Integration

Over 60% of new industrial display installations now include touch capability. The trend is driven by:

  • Reduced physical buttons and switches on CNC control panels
  • More intuitive operator interfaces
  • PCAP (projected capacitive) technology enabling multi-touch and glove operation

2.3 High-Brightness and Outdoor Displays

Displays with 1,000+ cd/m² brightness are increasingly demanded for outdoor and semi-outdoor industrial applications such as construction machinery, marine systems, and solar panel monitoring.
