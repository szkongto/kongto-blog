Press Release
*April 30, 2026, Shenzhen* — Shenzhen Jiangtu Technology Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "Jiangtu Technology") officially released the FANUC CNC Display LCD Upgrade Solution, providing a plug-and-play display upgrade path for global CNC numerical control systems and industrial automation equipment.

Company Overview

Jiangtu Technology is a technology-oriented enterprise focusing on industrial video display solutions, committed to providing high-reliability display upgrade and retrofit services for CNC numerical control systems and industrial automation equipment. The company's core business includes:

Business Area Main Products
CNC Display Upgrade FANUC CNC display LCD upgrade kits
Video Signal Conversion Industrial video signal converters
Industrial Control Display Industrial displays (high temperature resistance, anti-interference)

With years of R&D experience and a complete supply chain system, Jiangtu Technology helps customers achieve "old machine with new screen", significantly improving equipment visualization and maintenance efficiency.

Product Highlights

Item Description
Model FANUC A61L-0001-0093 LCD Display
Size Compatibility Retains original CRT mounting dimensions, adopts replacement installation, no need to modify equipment structure or recalibrate mechanical position
Signal Interface Uses HONDA 20-pin signal interface, perfectly compatible with FANUC 0i, 16i, 18i series CNC systems
Power Supply Uses DC 24V power supply, compatible with original CRT power design, eliminating additional power modifications
Display Performance Adopts high-brightness, wide-viewing-angle industrial-grade LCD, resolution upgraded to 1024×768, response time < 8 ms, significantly improving operation interface clarity
Environmental Adaptability Operating temperature range -20°C~+70°C, dustproof and shockproof, complies with ISO 16750 industrial environment standards
Plug-and-Play Completes signal and power transmission through a single 20-pin cable, installation time reduced by more than 70%

Market Significance

1. Extend Equipment Lifespan – Through the LCD replacement solution, outdated CRT displays can achieve image quality upgrades without replacing the entire machine, reducing capital expenditure.
2. Improve Operational Safety – High-resolution, wide-viewing-angle display reduces misoperation risks, complying with increasingly strict industrial safety regulations.
3. Accelerate Digital Transformation – Fully compatible with mainstream FANUC CNC systems, providing reliable front-end display support for smart manufacturing and Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT).
