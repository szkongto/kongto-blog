FANUC D9MM-11A (A61L-0001-0093) 9-Inch Monochrome Display Professional LCD Retrofit Solution

2026-05-03 Source: Kongto Technology

The D9MM-11A is a FANUC 9-inch monochrome display from an alternative numbering system, which actually corresponds to the classic A61L-0001-0093. On some older FANUC equipment nameplates, you may see "D9MM-11A" instead of "A61L-0001-0093" - they refer to the same display.

1. Relationship Between D9MM-11A and A61L-0001-0093

Many users are confused about these two part numbers. Here is a clear reference:

Numbering SystemModelNotes
FANUC Part NumberA61L-0001-0093International standard
Display Spec CodeD9MM-11AEarly internal code
CRT Tube ModelMDT947B-1A / 9DJ1May vary by batch
Common NameFANUC 9-inch Green ScreenIndustry standard

Simple understanding: D9MM-11A = A61L-0001-0093 = FANUC 9-inch monochrome display. All three refer to the same product.

2. Typical Applications of D9MM-11A

This display is commonly found in the following FANUC CNC systems:

  • FANUC Series 0-M — Milling machine/Machining center control system
  • FANUC OM-D — Early machining center system
  • FANUC 0-Mate — Compact milling system
  • FANUC Power Mate — Stepper motor control system

These systems still have a large number of units in operation in China, especially in precision parts processing plants, mold manufacturing enterprises, aerospace component workshops, university training centers, and vocational colleges.
