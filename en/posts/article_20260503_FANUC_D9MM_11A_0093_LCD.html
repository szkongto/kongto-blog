FANUC A61L_0001_0093 LCD Retrofit Guide

Published: 2026-05-03 | FANUC CNC Display Solutions

Product Overview

The A61L_0001_0093_LCD is a professional LCD retrofit kit designed to replace aging CRT displays in FANUC CNC systems. This 9-inch monochrome LCD solution maintains 100% compatibility with the original mounting dimensions and electrical interface, ensuring a seamless upgrade path.

Key Features

  • Direct replacement for original CRT display - no mechanical modifications required
  • 9-inch monochrome display with high-brightness LCD panel
  • Same mounting dimensions as original CRT unit
  • Industrial-grade design for 24/7 operation in harsh workshop environments
  • Wide power input: DC 24V compatible with original power supply
  • 2-year warranty for peace of mind

Display Comparison

The LCD retrofit provides significant advantages over the original CRT:

  • Brightness: LCD maintains consistent brightness; CRT dims over time
  • Lifespan: LCD lasts 50,000+ hours vs. CRT's 15-20 year average
  • Power efficiency: LCD consumes 60% less power
  • Heat generation: LCD runs cooler, reducing workshop temperature
  • Image quality: Sharper text, no flicker, no geometric distortion

Installation

Installation is straightforward and typically takes 15-30 minutes:

  1. Disconnect power and remove the old CRT display
  2. Mount the new LCD unit using the original mounting holes
  3. Connect the signal cable to the original interface
  4. Connect DC 24V power supply
  5. Power on and adjust OSD settings if needed

Compatibility

This LCD retrofit is compatible with:

  • FANUC 0i-Mate series
  • FANUC 0i-B/C/D series
  • FANUC 16i/18i/21i series
  • Other FANUC systems using A61L_0001_0093 type displays
