Background: Is Your Mazak Machine Still Running on a CRT Display?

If you own a Mazak CNC machine equipped with Mazatrol M-Plus, M1, M2, M32, QT, T-Plus, T2, T3, or T32 control systems, you're likely facing a critical problem — the original 14-inch color CRT monitor (model CD1472-D1M) is aging or has already failed.

Manufactured by Hitachi, this CRT display has been widely used in Mazak and Mitsubishi CNC systems since the 1990s. However, as a cathode ray tube monitor that has been out of production for over 20 years, it faces these fatal issues:

IssueImpact
CRT tube agingDim image, blurry focus, color distortion
Flyback transformer failureNo display; repair cost exceeds replacement
Component failureCapacitor leakage, PCB corrosion
Discontinued productionNew units unavailable; used parts unreliable
Bulky & heavyOccupies space; difficult to handle and install

A downed machine can lose more in production value per day than the cost of a monitor replacement.
